The Idea

The Idea for TheBookWagon originated when as first years our college began and we had to have books but obviously we got physical books which are difficult to carry and manage not only at college but also at home.

So seeing the usage of digital media in the todays world it is obvious that Digital books, PDFs are more and more prominent now so we decided to start a website where we would provide all the course books of Btech 1st, 2nd, 3rd and 4th year students of IITM in PDF format. Which would also help them to reduce time which they would use to find these books on the internet and surf a number of sites which would also lower their productivity.
